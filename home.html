On The House

Our App

We created this app to help students and people of lower socio economic class who are in need of financial relief. This app helps to lower prices of day-to-day goods that are essential for everyone.

Leaderboard

Leaderboard contains a list of all the donors who have participated in this program. The donor with the most contributions to the program will apear first. The list can be modified using the range modifier at the top to filter through the leaderboard.

Listings

The listings section holds what items are available for sale at the businesses. If you are one of our generous donors who would like to edit, delete or add items, you can navigate to the bottom of this page after you have logged in.

Login

Before viewing listings, please don't forget to login. You can create an account that is for seeing items only as a buyer. If you want to be one of our generous donors, you can create an account as a user, and if you would like to use the app and donate, you can create an account as both buyer and seller.

Profile

After creating an account, you can navigate to the profile section to change the details that you used to create the account initially.
